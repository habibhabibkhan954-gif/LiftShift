What is LiftShift?

A free, privacy-focused workout analytics tool. Connect Hevy, Strong, or Lyfta to get muscle heatmaps, plateau detection, set-by-set feedback, and AI-ready exports. Everything runs in your browser.

Does LiftShift store my workout data on its servers?

No. All analytics are computed locally in your browser. Your workout history never leaves your device. Data is cached in browser storage for faster reloads. See /privacy/.

Which apps are supported?

  • Hevy — API sync or CSV import
  • Strong — CSV import
  • Lyfta — CSV import

You can combine data from multiple apps into one unified dashboard. See How it works: Import & sync.

How does plateau detection work?

LiftShift compares a recent window of sessions to the previous window. If your strength change is between -3% and +2%, you get a Plateauing label. If both weight and reps are frozen across multiple sessions, it marks a static plateau and gives a specific suggestion — e.g. “add 1 rep or bump the weight 2.5 kg.” See How it works: Plateau detection.

Can LiftShift analyze my training with AI?

Yes. Pick a timeframe, optionally select analysis modules (junk volume audit, structural balance, joint health, etc.), and LiftShift generates a structured prompt + data. Paste into any AI tool. See How it works: AI export.

What PRs does LiftShift track?

Three kinds: Gold PRs (all-time bests), Silver PRs (best in the last 2 months), and premature PRs (big jumps you couldn’t sustain). Also tracks PR droughts and frequency. See How it works: PRs.

What is set-by-set feedback?

Open any past workout and LiftShift analyzes each set relative to the previous one across 19 scenarios. Each set gets a short badge (“Normal Fatigue,” “Too Aggressive,” “Good Reset”) with a tooltip and suggestion. See How it works: Set-by-set feedback.

Why might my charts look wrong?

  • Exercise names differ across sessions (e.g. “Bench Press” vs “Barbell Bench”).
  • Units changed (kg vs lb) in the source export.
  • Warmup sets are included or excluded differently than you expect.
  • Missing sessions or incomplete logs reduce trend signal.

See How it works: Troubleshooting for more.

Is LiftShift a coaching app?

No. LiftShift is analytics for your training logs. It gives data-driven feedback but does not generate training programs and is not medical advice.
